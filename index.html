Bushnell Golf GPS Map Update

Keeping your Bushnell Golf GPS device updated with the latest course maps ensures accurate yardage, reliable tracking, and enhanced play on every round. Whether you're using the Phantom 2, iON Edge, or other models, this guide helps you update with ease.

Why Map Updates Matter

  • Ensure your golf course layout and hole distances are accurate
  • Get updates for new courses or redesigned holes
  • Improve shot accuracy and course strategy
  • Fix map glitches or firmware bugs

What You Need

  • Your Bushnell Golf GPS device (e.g., Phantom 2, iON Edge, Excel)
  • Bushnell Golf app installed on your smartphone
  • Active internet connection (Wi-Fi or data)
  • USB cable (for desktop software if needed)

How to Update Bushnell Golf Maps

Via Bushnell Golf App (Bluetooth)

  • Launch the Bushnell Golf app and sign in
  • Pair your device via Bluetooth
  • Navigate to Device Settings > Update Course Maps
  • Select available updates and follow prompts

Via PC (Bushnell Update Manager)

  • Download and install the Bushnell Golf Update Manager from the official site
  • Connect your GPS via USB
  • Run the software, check for updates, and install maps

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Device not pairing: Restart Bluetooth and relaunch the app
  • Map update stuck: Reboot the GPS device and try again
  • No update found: Ensure the app and firmware are up to date

Conclusion

With your Bushnell Golf GPS maps up to date, you can play confidently knowing your yardages and course layouts are accurate. Use the mobile app or desktop method to stay on top of every game.
